# Efficient Multi-Task Representation Learning

## Abstract
We propose a lightweight representation learning method that improves average accuracy by 3.4 points across three benchmarks while reducing latency by 22%.

## Method
Our method introduces a shared encoder and task-specific adapters with a consistency loss.

## Experiments
We compare against two strong baselines and report top-1 accuracy on three datasets.

## Ablation
We analyze the impact of each component and run variants without adapter layers.

## Limitations
Our approach has not been evaluated for multilingual setups.
